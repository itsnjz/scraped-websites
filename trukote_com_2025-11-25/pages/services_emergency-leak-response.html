Overview

When a leak strikes, every minute counts. Tru-kote’s Emergency Leak Response team is on call 24/7 to protect your property from water damage. Our experts quickly locate the source, provide immediate temporary fixes, and outline permanent repair solutions — all while keeping you informed every step of the way. Whether it’s a small drip or a major breach, we’re here to secure your roof when you need us most.

Key Services

Our Emergency Leak Response covers everything from immediate leak containment to long‑term solutions, ensuring your roof stays secure and your operations uninterrupted.
